Bonjour, I am Annick Vavasseur, A passionate painter based in Paris.

Born in France in 1986, Annick initially studied art history, and the techniques of trompe-l'œil. She specializes in the imitation of wood, marble, and stone, and also creates enchanting scenes through trompe-l'œil windows.

She continued her studies at the Panthéon-Sorbonne University, in Paris, where she earned a Master's degree in Visual Arts' research. Constantly refining her craft, she regularly participates in workshops at the Ateliers des Beaux-Arts de la Ville de Paris, Paris Ateliers, and the Louvre Museum.

Her paintings transport us to boundless worlds where magic, dreamlike imagery, and surrealism often intertwine.

"There is so much to learn about painting, it's a wonderful lifelong adventure", she claims.

About her Art

Her creative journey blends tradition with modern expression. Her paintings conceal entire worlds beneath their visible surfaces. What appears as a single composition is often multiple works layered upon one another—an artistic palimpsest where only the final stratum meets the eye. This technique emerges from an irrepressible creative impulse to both reveal and conceal.

Each canvas holds a secret dialogue between what is shown and what remains hidden—a treasure of expressions captured in time, with only the uppermost layer pierced by light. This tension between visibility and concealment defines her artistic practice, inviting viewers to sense the depths beyond what is immediately perceived.

Inspiration

Through every brushstroke, she translates her innermost inspiration onto canvas, creating portals where vision becomes tangible emotion for others to experience.

Technique

She blends traditional methods with modern approaches in her technique, crafting layers of depth and emotion that resonate beyond the visual plane.

Vision

Her vision transforms fleeting moments into permanent expressions, each painting preserving a singular experience that can never be replicated.

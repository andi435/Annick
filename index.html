About My Art

My creative journey blends tradition with modern expression. I invite you to explore the process behind every painting. My paintings conceal entire worlds beneath their visible surfaces. What appears as a single composition is often multiple works layered upon one another—an artistic palimpsest where only the final stratum meets the eye. This technique emerges from an irrepressible creative impulse to both reveal and conceal.

Each canvas holds a secret dialogue between what is shown and what remains hidden—a treasure of expressions captured in time, with only the uppermost layer pierced by light. This tension between visibility and concealment defines my artistic practice, inviting viewers to sense the depths beyond what is immediately perceived.
